Logo and Branding

Because I am heavily into music and playing an instrument is one of my biggest hobbies, I think I’m going to go for something towards that area. It would most likely be related to something of the string instrument since my main instrument is a guitar. It could be also something like the bass or ukelele. Though there is already an app or company that embodies this pretty well, it could make a better change and embody more things and more instruments for people to learn and enjoy playing music. The purpose of this company will be to provide assistance in playing/learning music and the instruments that can be played with recording instruments, the guitar being the most prominent and familiar ones. It would give the user a feeling that this is a string instrument library, as it provides tips, lessons, tabs, and options to record or loop, and etc.

Guitar or string instrument being a common one, and the one already being used the most, I’ll be editing and messing around with the shape of a guitar pick, and try to change up the colors or the drawings that can be added into the logo itself to symbolize and show what this app is for.
